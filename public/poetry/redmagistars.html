red magi stars

Sons compete for fathers' jobs
Sunshine greets us -
After the drive
Retirement beckons -
Retrenchment's loss
Captives in our fields of grey
Cubicles we sit all day
We dream up ways
To kill the boss
To kill the rival
To kill that coffee maid
Who always serves us coffee last
-When it's cold it's drunk too fast

Through streaks of smoke
At smoking break
Dreamily we gaze
At secretaries
And coffee maids
Sipping from
Coffee coffee maid made

Till five o' clock sounds
To bleeping horns
A setting sun
Our only solace
Behind the wall of stars -
Red tail lights
Our red magi stars
Guide the way to grimy bars
Or grimy streets
Harbouring our sleeping homes

We are but footsteps in our children's dreams
Ceaseless tales devoid of themes
