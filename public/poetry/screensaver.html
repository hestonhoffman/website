screensaver

Into the tropical screensaver
Dreamily I gaze
At fish and sea anemone
And bubbles making bubble sounds
I float in the blue void
Occasional backstroke
Catch a ride on a fish
Pop bubbles

The phone rings
Attempts to return me
I will not.

I watch
As the office empties
And decide it's getting cold
I say goodbye to the fish
Pop one last bubble
And float towards the face
Staring back at me
I flatten my nose against the screen

The office lights go out

The dark glow of the screen
Illuminates an empty stare
Sitting in an office chair
