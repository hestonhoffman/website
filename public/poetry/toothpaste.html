toothpaste man

A speck of toothpaste on his tie
removes him from the others.

the speck of toothpaste
slowly grows as a result of the radiation beams from that glowing monitor
grows and slowly
grows legs and arms
and sparkling silver
sparkling smile
leaps from the tie
dances around the room
accidentally kicking over the office manikins
in its adaptation
of the 'sugar plum fairies'
and then growing
and duplicating itself
until there is a toothpaste
man behind each manikin
they act out the scenes from
the latest Broadway show
here lifting plastic arms
or thumping plastic chests
until they grow tired
and a sudden realisation
whispers dry air from
a long opened window
nothing stirs
but only parches manikin throats
and freezes each
slowly
in mid yawn
