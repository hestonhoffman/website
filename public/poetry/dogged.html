dogged

Watching his legs twitch,
I realise that Rover is having that same dream again:
the one in which he is wearing a suit and tie
like some backwards leash
and that he is standing at that infernal
water-cooler
on his hind legs,
when a familiar smell wafts in
from a distant cubicle,
of far too much perfume,
and a feint rumble beneath his paws
suggests that Deloris is approaching,
and that soon
he will be forced to make small-talk
while trying to avoid her
sweaty armpit patches.
