office vampire

On the way to the gents
I met Simon:
the office vampire.
"Working late, too?" I asked.
"Yes", he said
and split his lips
in a smile,
barely baring
Cain's canines.

As I turned,
I wondered
when those calcium pins
would next pierce
the damsel flesh,
when next gauge
the rushing pulse.
A question answered
With sharp pain and
Dizziness.
