award winning boerewors

Everywhere that glean.
Shimmering along the isles.
Conglomerate children
bred on conveyer belts.
Over loud speakers
the voice of big brother
stresses the wrong syllables,
heralds the sale of
award winning boerewors
in between commands
for the security guards
to change their positions
'for the safety of the customers.'
A man in a fish costume
hands out balloons to
the brethren of dazed
frozen food pedestrians.

A belch
of fetid air
gusts through the rubber
swing doors
from the passage
where the perishables are kept.

In a cardboard office,
a disgruntled potbelly
writes an order with
a troll-head-capped pencil

The troll has pink hair.
